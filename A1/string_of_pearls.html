    senecio rowleyanus

    String of Pearls


    about :

    The String of Pearls is a beautiful succulent with tiny green pearl-like leaves along many of its stems. These stems hang down along the sides of the pot, which can extend potentially up to 2 ft. The leaves of this succulent have the chance of blooming white flowers that have the scent of cinnamon.


    growth & care :

    This type of succulent does not need a lot of care, and if it's properly cared for, they can live up to about 5 years. Although it is easy to regrow another by cutting some of its stem and placing it into soil, allowing it to root and grow.


    Make sure that the succulent gets multiple hours of direct sunlight each day. Additionally, during the growing season of spring & summer, allow the soil to be moist. This can be reduced in fall & winter. It is also important to give it fertilizer during the growing season, and repot it every year at the start of spring.
