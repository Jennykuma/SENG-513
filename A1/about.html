    • About Succulents

    what is a succulent ?

    Succulents are basically plants that store water in their tissues (leaves, stems, or both). There are many types of succulents that come in a variety of shapes, sizes and colours. Some even have interesting features that make them stand out from others. Some succulents can be found in deserts (cacti) or even in cold mountains, jungles, or seaside cliffs. They can tolerate periods of drought for as long as a couple months and most succulents grow best under bright light.


    two types of succulents :

    › hardy

    Succulents that can be grown outdoors year round

    › not hardy

    Succulents that can be grown outdoors in pots during spring and summer, but need to be indoors during cold weather such as in winter


    growing succulents :

    To obtain the best growth and colour for your succulent, they will need very bright light (put them beside a window where the sun will shine). In humid areas, they will need protection from the midday sun asa it may be too hot for them. Different types of succulents will need at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight.


    Succulents will also need to be planted in areas that never stay wet. Make sure not to overwater them! They can be planted in areas with fast drainage such as dry climates or wall hanging pots. To improve the drainage, you can add coarse sand or grit to the soil.
